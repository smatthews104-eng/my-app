Research compound
PEPS: why it matters for you
PEPS is the KLOW blend, 4 peptides targeting everything ACL and meniscus recovery needs. Log your daily dose on the Today tab.
PEPS and Retatrutide work through completely different pathways. No interference with each other.
Water retention from GHK-Cu makes the scale unreliable. Track tape measurements and photos, not just the number.
BPC-157 heals gut lining, which may reduce Retatrutide GI side effects. Genuine bonus benefit.
Both PEPS and Reta are research / investigational only. Clear with a clinician. Use proper sterile injection technique.
The 4 peptides, plain English
BPC-157
Tissue repair specialist
Grows new blood vessels into injury sites, signals healing factors to damaged tissue, and protects the gut lining. Think of it as the crew that builds the road so supplies reach the repair site.
Cartilage and tendon repair data. Drives angiogenesis, the critical early step in ACL graft integration. May reduce Reta GI nausea.
TB-500
Systemic healing activator
Helps cells migrate to injury sites, promotes flexibility and remodeling in connective tissue, supports blood vessel growth body-wide.
Supports connective tissue around the ACL and meniscus. With BPC-157 this pair is called the Wolverine Stack.
GHK-Cu
Collagen architect
A copper peptide that influences thousands of genes involved in tissue repair and collagen production. The largest component in the blend.
Drives collagen in tendons and joint tissue. Overlaps with your collagen supplement, so halve that on PEPS days. Main cause of water retention, use tape not scale.
KPV
Inflammation controller
Derived from alpha-MSH. Calms the inflammatory cascade that, unchecked after surgery, delays healing and causes chronic pain.
Reduces low-grade inflammation after ACL surgery. Warning: less swelling does not mean the knee is fine. Track the pain number carefully.
What changes with PEPS
Plan adjustments
Reduce collagen supplement on PEPS days. GHK-Cu covers that mechanism. Half dose or skip.
Slightly faster phase progression possible if pain stays consistently 0 to 2. Pain rule still governs.
Reduced swelling does not mean healed. KPV dampens the signal. Track the pain number.
PEPS timing for knee: 30 to 60 min before lower-body sessions for the best joint healing stimulus.
Scale unreliable on PEPS. Use tape (waist, hips, thigh) and progress photos as real markers.
Protocol: typically 5 days on / 2 off, or daily. Half dose week 1 to assess tolerance. Cycles run 8 to 12 weeks.
